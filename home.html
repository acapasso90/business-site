Animal Crossing New Horizon's Critterpedia built using React and the ACNH API. By default it displays fish that are currently available in the Northern Hemisphere during the user's current month and hour. Dropdowns are available to change the time and month and there are buttons for each hemisphere. Bugs and Deep Sea creatures can be accessed by clicking the respective icons on the top-left of the header, matching the game's UI. Each bug/fish/deep sea creature can be clicked to learn more information such as sell price, time frame and months available, and rarity.

Additionally, card prices and details are able to be searched by either card name, card subtype, card set, or card rarity. Prices are fed from TCGPlayer and buttons are provided to go directly to seller listings for each card.
